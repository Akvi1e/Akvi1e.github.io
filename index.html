LEARN HOW TO BRUSH YOUR TEETH

A quick and fun guide for kids!

Let's Get Started

It’s time to brush our teeth together!

We need to brush them every morning and evening for 2 minutes! It’s important
to remove all the germs that can hurt your teeth by making little holes

Use a toothbrush with soft bristles designed for kids

Put a little dot of a fluoride toothpaste

About the size of a pea

Open your mouth wide! And start with the teeth at the back of your mouth

Brush in little circles and work your way to the teeth in the front

Don’t forget to brush the gums! Move the brush up and down gently in short strokes.
Brush the top, front and insides of every tooth

When you are done be sure to spit out all the toothpaste. Don’t swallow, it will upset your tummy!

If you learn to brush your teeth right, you will have a beautiful and healthy smile!
